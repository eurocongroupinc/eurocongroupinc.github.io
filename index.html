About Eurocon Group Industries

Since 2007, Eurocon Group Industries Pvt. Ltd. has delivered high-quality engineering and IT projects across manufacturing, infrastructure and commercial sectors. We combine field expertise with software and cloud solutions to deliver measurable outcomes: increased uptime, safer operations and improved efficiency.

Our mission

Deliver safe, efficient and sustainable engineering projects alongside practical IT solutions.

Our Services

Comprehensive engineering and IT services tailored to industrial and enterprise needs.

Civil & Structural Engineering

Structural design, foundation engineering, industrial building construction, retrofits and civil consultancy.

  • Site surveys & feasibility
  • Structural drawings & BOQs
  • Project supervision

Mechanical & Piping

Mechanical systems design, HVAC, piping layouts, equipment selection and fabrication oversight.

  • Equipment sizing & selection
  • Piping isometrics & stress analysis
  • Fabrication & erection support

Electrical & Instrumentation

Power distribution, switchgear, instrumentation and control strategies for industrial plants.

  • MV/LV system design
  • Control panel & wiring diagrams
  • Instrument specification & calibration

Automation & Control

  • Control system architecture
  • PLC/HMI programming
  • On-site commissioning

Software & Cloud

  • Web & mobile apps
  • Cloud infrastructure & DevOps
  • IoT dashboards & analytics

IT Support & Cybersecurity
